AI-Based Traffic Violation Detection

Our smart surveillance software detects speeding vehicles, lane violations, and red light jumping in real-time. It integrates with existing traffic cameras and generates actionable alerts for authorities.

Core Capabilities

  • 🚗 Speed Violation Detection using frame timing
  • 🛑 Red Light Violation Analysis
  • 🔍 ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition)
  • 📡 Alert auto-sent to traffic department via email/API
  • 📁 Reports saved with date, time, vehicle image & plate

Workflow

  1. Video feed is analyzed by AI
  2. Vehicles exceeding speed threshold are flagged
  3. Number plate is extracted via OCR
  4. Alert is created & forwarded to authority
